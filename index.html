How to Play Word Drip

Form words using the letters dripping on the grid. New letters drip down every 10 seconds.

Rules:

  • Words must be at least 3 letters long
  • You can only use each letter once
  • Letters are arranged in rows of 7 (maximum 3 rows)
  • Game ends when time runs out, you form 15 words, or the grid fills up

Scoring:

  • 3-letter words: 3 points
  • 4-letter words: 4 points
  • 5-letter words: 10 points
  • 6+ letter words: 15 points

Daily Streak:

Play every day to build your streak! Miss a day and you'll have to start over.
